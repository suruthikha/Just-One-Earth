Challenges

Participate in some of our challenges and become further involved in healing our Earth.

Level 1 Challenges

Ditch the Car

Car emissions thoroughly harm the environment, causing global warming. Walk, ride a bike, or take public transport instead.

Reusable Water Bottle

Billions of plastic water bottles are thrown away every year instead of being recycled. Simply refill your water bottle to help the environment.

Recycle Something

Recycling items directly reduces pollution. It reduces the amount of waste that is sent to landfills. This also conserves some of our natural resources. Many household items can be recycled.

Level 2 Challenges

Clean Your Community

Do an act of service by helping clean up around your community. Committing to picking up pieces of trash can help to improve an area. It makes it a nicer place to live and helps wildlife.

Plant a Tree

Trees give off the oxygen we need to breathe. They support wildlife and allow us to produce many products we frequently use. Trees are a necessary and important part of our lives. You can also donate to causes that support reforestation.

Have a Vegetarian Day

Since its difficult for some the go all the way, have a vegetarian day once in a while. Vegetarianism significantly cuts down your carbon footprint and decreases animal cruelty.
