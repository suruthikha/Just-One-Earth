What is Climate Change, and what causes it?







According to NASA, climate change is"Any long-term change in Earth's climate, or in the climate of a region or city. This includes warming, cooling and changes besides temperature."



And according to the accepted theory, climate change is caused by the Greenhouse effect. Greenhouse gasses stop heat from leaving the Earth's atmosphere, like a giant greenhouse. Examples of greenhouse gasses are carbon dioxide and methane.
